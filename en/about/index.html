About EnglishTrainer

EnglishTrainer is a free online vocabulary trainer. The goal is simple: help you learn and retain words in small, painless sessions — for free. You see a word and pick the correct translation out of four options, which builds memory faster than reading lists.

The trainer has about 4,600 words grouped by CEFR levels — A1 to C1. You can study in both directions (English → Russian and back), review your mistakes, play 10-word rounds, and estimate your level with an adaptive test.

Why it's built this way

We deliberately kept it light and fast: it runs right in your browser, needs no sign-up, and your progress is saved on your device. After the first visit, the page even opens offline.

FAQ

Is the trainer free?
Yes, completely free and no sign-up required.
Do I need to install anything?
No, it runs in the browser. You can optionally install it as an app (PWA) on your phone.
How many words are there?
About 4,600 English words across CEFR levels A1 to C1.
Is my progress saved?
Yes, stats and history are saved in your browser. Cross-device sync is coming later.
How do I find my level?
Take the adaptive level test — it estimates your level in 1–2 minutes.
